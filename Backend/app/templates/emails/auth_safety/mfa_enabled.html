Your have successfully enabled multi-factor authentication (MFA) in your account.

What does this mean?

You will be prompted to provide a one-time-password (OTP) along with your account password when signing in or when making changes to your account's credentials (such as changing your password or account's email address).

This increases the safety of your account by protecting you from phishing attempts or brute-force attacks.

We highly recommend setting a recovery email address in your account, in case you have not already done so. This can be done under "Account Settings".

A recovery email can help you re-gain access to your account in the case you forget your password or if you lose access to your primary email account.
